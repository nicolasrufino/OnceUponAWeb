Heels, for Him

The Rick Owens’ Kiss Heel is a rebellion. It is not only chunky and bold, but also defies the societal norms dictating that only women can use heels. It’s not just a shoe—it’s a statement: identity isn’t about rules, it’s about breaking them. Rick Owens wears this shoe constantly to combat social standards. Wear whatever pleases you.

Architect of Identity

 Rick Owens is a provocateur. Inspired by punk, his designs are a middle finger to conformity and social standards . He doesn’t care about trends—he cares about substance. His message? Identity isn’t given—it’s taken. Be you. A perfect example of this is when he dropped out of college. Why did he drop out? He realized that fashion was being taught as a fixed concept, and he decided to follow his own path. A true architect of identity.

Wear Your Rebellion

Rick Owens designs are unique. He doesn’t need color to express himself. Monochromatic, audacious and innovative, scream POWER. Fashion isn’t just what you wear — but who you are. Own it. Own your Identity. Find it. Don’t be afraid. No-one else can be you for you.
