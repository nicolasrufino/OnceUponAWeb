An Inspiration of Identity

Rick Owens is an inspiration to me.
His designs derive from punk.
Which is why his designs are a middle finger to conformity and social standards.
He doesnt care about trends, but substance.
Owens has had a big influence on how I think and view the world.
He is able to deliver this message through visuals and clothing.
In life I have learned that identity isnt given, its taken.

Rick Owens designs are unique.
