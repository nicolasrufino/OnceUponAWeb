Initiation

This journey began with a single pair of boots.
Inspired by Rick Owens’ Kiss Heels, I stepped outside my comfort zone.
I had always gravitated toward colorful sneakers, but his dark, punk aesthetic drew me in.
At first, I hesitated—where I come from, such drastic style changes are rare.
But the moment I put them on, I felt like a new version of myself.
I loved everything—the high heel, the chunky gothic look, the bold statement.
With these boots, I took a step into a new world—and I haven’t looked back.

Adaptation

After that first step, the next ones came naturally.
The boots felt like a part of me—like they had always been there.
I wore them everywhere, with everything.
I experimented with new accessories, new aesthetics.
With each choice, I felt more confident, more powerful.
I felt like me.
I felt like Rick Owens.

Sharing Your Rebellion

This journey led me to share it with close friends
Those who embodied rebellion and embraced the unconventional.
People that were not afraid to be different, to be unique, to be themselves.
People that would cut, sew, and paint as a form of expression.
Now, I thrive in this environment, where I can be myself, where I can be unique, where I can be me.
